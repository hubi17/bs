Hexadecimal Number

\b0[xX][0-9a-fA-F]+\b

  • Assert position at a word boundary
  • Match the character "0" literally
  • Match a single character out of the list: one of the characters "xX"
  • Match a single character out of the list: in the range between 0 and 9, or in the range between a and f, or in the range between A and F
    • Between one and unlimited times, as many times as possible, giving back as needed (greedy)
  • Assert position at a word boundary
