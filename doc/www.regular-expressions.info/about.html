About The Author

Jan Goyvaerts is Chief Software Designer at his own software company Just Great Software. Just Great Software develops and publishes various software packages including PowerGREP, RegexBuddy and EditPad Pro.

PowerGREP is a knowledge worker's Swiss army knife for searching through, filtering and retrieving information from piles of files using regular expressions. RegexBuddy is your perfect assistant for working with regular expressions, making it easy to create, test and save regexes. EditPad Pro is a popular text editor with particularly strong support for regular expressions.

Jan is an avid blogger. He writes three blogs. Regex Guru is his blog about regular expressions, on which he is a world-class expert. Micro-ISV.asia is his software business blog, where he writes about his own business as well as the software industry in general. Finally, Phuket.ME is his personal blog, where he writes about what's happening in his life and the island he lives on with his family.
